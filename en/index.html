Ecoinstrument is a leading integrator of electro-analytical instrumentation and equipment for environmental monitoring. We are focused on projects implementation for ecological, industrial, scientific and educational fields.

Our hallmark is accurate understanding and comprehensive approach to customers needs.

We have direct sales and service offices in Russia, CIS and Europe, as well as a wide network of representatives and service technicians. Our core competencies include widely varying activities such as:

● Ecological research and instrumental equipping of laboratories and environmental expeditions

● Supply and start-adjustment on water treatment plants

● Equipment for food processing, pharmaceutical, automotive industries and scientific institutions

Together with our local offices and dealers we assist you with the best support, services, parts and consumables, especially designed and selected to secure the most accurate performance of your laboratory analysis for years to come.
