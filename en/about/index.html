Ecoinstrument is a leading integrator of electro-analytical instrumentation and equipment for environmental monitoring.

We are focused on projects implementation for ecological, industrial, scientific and educational fields.

Our hallmark is accurate understanding and comprehensive approach to customers needs.

We have direct sales and service offices in Russia, CIS and Europe, as well as a wide network of representatives and service technicians. Our core competencies include widely varying activities such as:

  • Ecological research and instrumental equipping of laboratories and environmental expeditions
  • Supply and start-adjustment on water treatment plants
  • Equipment for food processing, pharmaceutical, automotive industries and scientific institutions

We are always passionately involved in the issues and concerns of our customers, understanding that to find the right decision is the cause of our professional honor.

We provide technical training seminars and publish technical information in support of our customers' efforts to understand and improve the operations of their facilities.

Our team of experts has many years of national and international experience in equipment and solutions for lab/ fieldwork water analysis and for ecology monitoring, for beverages, beer and food quality control.

Range of products we offer is wide enough to meet any challenge which stands before our customer, from simple pocket pH-meter to a fully completed quality control lab set-up.

Our mission: supplying only the highest quality equipment available, providing dependable service after the sale, and having a knowledgeable staff that is always available to assist our customers in order to meet the highest State and the international standards.

Company history

Founded in 1993, Ecoinstrument is dedicated to bringing engineering and products together.  Our systems approach looks at a customer application or problem as a whole.  We then recommend products designed to work together to not only provide a solution but also allow for ease of use and operator flexibility. 

1993 Ecoinstrument starts producing inexpensive pH and ISE electrodes and meters. Since that time high quality is a requirement for any product we produce or supply. 

1995 Ecoinstrument launches import business as the premier supplier of the world famous brands like HANNA Instruments, HACH-Lange, WTW, Eijkelkamp, KERN, Haws, GSSI, and others, offering a wide selection of laboratory and process instrumentation as well as related process equipment. 

1999 Ecoinstrument opens it`s local offices all over the Russia and CIS: 

  • 1999 — Nizhny Novgorod, 

  • 2000 —  Ekaterinburg, 

  • 2005 — Kyiv (Ukraine), 

  • 2012 — Almaty (Kazakhstan).

Now, with representation in Russia, CIS and Germany, your service and support needs are covered, for both laboratory instruments and complicated projects. Our dealer network provides personalized support that leads to more efficiency and greater productivity for your laboratory needs. As a result, we offer local service & support, where you need it, when you need it.

Nowadays Ecoinstrument combined years of experience together with continuous customer feedback, enabling to supply it`s clients wide range of laboratory instrumentation and enviromental projects implementation.

Together with our local offices and dealers we assist you with the best support, services, parts and consumables, especially designed and selected to secure the most accurate performance of your laboratory analysis for years to come.
