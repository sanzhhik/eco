Laboratory equipment

Ecoinstrument a Russian leading supplier of analytical instrumentation, has a network of 60 subdealers in 25 regions of the country and offers a great variety of products to its customers. The wide range of electrochemical analyzers (pH, conductivity, dissolved oxygen), spectrophotometers, turbidity meters, COD, BOD analyzers as well as industrial safety equipment form a complete solution for any modern lab involved in different types of research.

Industrial Solutions

Ecoinstrument offers a portfolio of analyzers and systems designed to effectively meet the demands and challenges of drinking water and wastewater on-line measurement. From smarter aeration to advanced filtration to chemical-free disinfection, Ecoinstrument's experts evaluate the current demands of customers and help them find the right solutions for their application. Through the brands that have been trusted for decades, Ecoinstrumentoffers hundreds of solutions backed by a comprehensive, integrated portfolio of services designed to ensure that water and wastewater treatment equipment keeps running at its best.

Service

EcoInstrument Service Center provides our customers with a technical, methodological and metrological support of all equipment supplied. Our skilled engineers are ready to provide you with any useful information. If you need consumables, trainings, warranty post-warranty repair - the only what is necessary, is to contact our service center. Our specialists are making translation of manuals and assay procedures, are engaged in certification and verification of measuring equipment, conducting training seminars. EcoInstrument is authorized by the worldwide manufacturers for carrying out commissioning and repair works.

Warehouse

Our company has its own warehouse, so a large quantity of goods is always available for instant order. The warehouse is equipped for storage of temperature-sensitive goods and has structures and facilities, convenient for unloading and loading. Order selection is carried out in a short time, keeping track of expiration dates and timing verification. Also, for our key customers we have a system of planned long-term storage.

Delivery

We also offer delivery services. Our logistics department consists of highly qualified specialists, provides various solutions for delivery and performs many different operations, such as customs clearance, licensing, TRCU certification, etc.
