New on-Line laser turbidity meters

We're proud to present new turbidity meters HACH TU5.

Absolutely new advances 360° x 90°optics
HACH TU5 turbidity meter utilizes unique optics, that sences more than any other turbidity meter, exhibits better precision at lowest range and minimal diffirences between parallel measurements.

Ecxellent confidence of laboratory and online measurements
Now you exclude any uncertainty between results thanks to identical 360° x 90°optics in both instruments.

Turbidity measurements became faster
TU5 instruments display shortast responce time due to 1) reducing by 98% of surface that needs cleaning and 2) sealed calibration vials and excluded need for silicon oilng.
Smaller volume of flowing sample means that you'll detect any changes in turbidity virtually instantly.

Light source
Class 2 Laser 2 device with 650 nm (EPA) or 850 nm (ISO) laser LED

Measurement range
EPA:
0 - 700 NTU / FNU / TE/F / FTU
0 - 100 mg/l
0 - 175 EBC
ISO:
0 - 1000 NTU / FNU / TE/F / FTU
0 - 100 mg/l
0 - 250 EBC

Measurement error
±2 % reading value or ±0,01 NTU (larger value) in range 0 - 40 NTU;
±10 % reading in range 40 - 1000 NTU
for primary formazin standard (@25°C)

Resolution
0,0001 NTU / FNU / TE/F / FTU / EBC / mg/l

Units
NTU; FNU; TE/F; FTU; EBC; mg/l with sample specific calibration

Ambient temperature
10 … 40 °C

Sample temperature
4  … 70 °C
